SATURN

Sometimes called 'the jewel of the solar system', Saturn is the second largest planet on earth after jupiter. It is mostly made of helium and there's no rigid land to stand on saturn unlike earth. Saturn is known for it's prominent ring system. Below are some facts about saturn.

Facts about Saturn
  • Its beautiful rings are not solid. They are made up of bits of ice, dust and rock.
  • The rings are huge but thin. The main rings could almost go from Earth to the moon. Yet, they are less than a kilometer thick.
  • Saturn could float in water because it is mostly made of gas.
  • Saturn goes around the Sun very slowly. A year on Saturn is more than 29 Earth years.
  • Saturn is named after the Roman god of wealth and agriculture and father of Jupiter.
